The PCI board is attacked to the motherboard were information can be placed inside.

The hard drive is the memory of the computer. It stores information and files that you computer can use in the future. It also tracks your history and your recordings so you can access it later.







The DVD is the part of the computer that allows the digital video disc to be read.
